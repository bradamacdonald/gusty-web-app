GUSTY

Gusty does not collect or store personal information on any server. Saved locations and settings are stored locally on your device using browser localStorage and are never transmitted to Everyday Designer. Gusty uses Vercel Analytics to collect anonymous page view data including device type, country, and pages visited. No personally identifiable information is collected. Third-party services used: Open-Meteo, Mapbox, GeoNames, Buy Me a Coffee — each governed by their own privacy policies.
